What Is the Enterprise Application Development Process?

The process of enterprise app development is divided into three stages. The kick-off stage is :

  1. Presenting your requirements.

    2. We go through and thoroughly analyse the data provided and build up a team. We choose the experts who can quickly and effectively complete the project within your stipulated time frame.

  2. Technical Requirement

    3. We prepare the technical documentation, and create a basic design of the app. You as our customer is 100% involved in the process of development. We take into account your opinion and design ideas to build the application to suit your expectations and vision.

  3. App Development

    4. SquallTec uses Scrum methodology, so you get updates every two weeks. Next, the app goes through User Acceptance Testing till the desired results are achieved.

  4. Monitoring and Implementation

    5. SquallTec then continuously monitors the application after implementation. There is a team of experts who fixes bugs, if any, and provides general customer support.
